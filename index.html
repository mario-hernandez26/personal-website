About

I'm a Systems & Machine Learning Engineer with a passion for building scalable ML systems, distributed computing infrastructure, and real-time market intelligence platforms. My work spans from low-level signal processing and transformer architectures to high-level distributed systems and blockchain protocols.

Currently working on independent research in real-time market intelligence, building large-scale systems for multi-exchange data ingestion and GPU-accelerated forecasting. I've also contributed to research in 5G LDPC decoding, software-defined radio, computational biology, and bird-call classification.
